Lily Yan

Artist · Writer · Photographer

My practice moves between photography, installation, and documentary work — drawn to the spaces where memory becomes material and belonging resists easy definition.

I hold an MFA in Experimental & Documentary Arts from Duke University. My thesis project, This is (not) Home, is a multimedia installation exploring an ancestral village near Shanghai through image, text, and object.
